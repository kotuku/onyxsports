About Us

ONYX was founded through a passion for sports and a desire to bring innovative team wear to everyone.

We specialise in manufacturing customised sports apparel to ensure you look and feel your best your arena.

We manufacture teamwear for a variety of sports including rugby, hockey, netball, soccer, basketball, as well as others. We also have a range of corporate apparel.

Our focus:

Design - we specialise in providing cutting edge designs to ensure you look your best. We have an arsenal of designs on hand for you to select from.

Performance - Our products utilise technically advanced materials to ensure you feel dry and cool in the heat of competition.

Delivery - ensuring timely delivery is paramount to all of our orders. Our standard lead time is 4 weeks for Sublimated Items as we manufacture all of our products from scratch. If you do require faster, please let us know your requirements and we can see what we can do to help.

Please contact us and let us know how we can assist with your apparel requirements!
